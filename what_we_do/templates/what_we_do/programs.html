{% trans 'Our supporting programs' %}

{% blocktrans %} The Association, always guided by the needs of its members in order to offer them the best possible, organizes various programs. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}In collaboration with Psychologist Mrs. Andri Christoudia, it provides support, education and empowerment services to both the sufferers themselves and their families. {% endblocktrans %}

  • {% trans 'Individual/family psychological support meetings' %}
  • {% blocktrans %}Parents' School: Various workshops are organized that aim to provide knowledge and skills to parents so that they can empower and support the child with their health issues and so that they can live with confidence and joy, how we communicate with our child and the family environment, preparation of parents and the child for surgery, etc. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% trans 'Workshops for sufferers themselves for empowerment and self-confidence' %}
  • {% trans 'Experiential workshops with the participation of parents and sufferers.' %}
  • {% trans 'Educational/informational lectures' %}

{% blocktrans %} The Association implements a financial support program for its members who travel abroad for their child's heart surgery as many times as necessary.

It also supports financially needy member families every Christmas and Easter or if for any other reason the need arises. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Public information and awareness campaigns are organized for Congenital Heart Diseases through the media, on the Website, on Facebook, through the information brochure, Congenital Heart Disease Week is organized from February 7-14 of each year, We participate in conferences in and outside Cyprus, etc. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}In collaboration with Pediatric Cardiologists and other organizations, various programs are organized with the ultimate goal of improving the quality of life of people with Congenital Heart Disease, such as: {% endblocktrans %}

  • {% trans 'Exercise and Congenital Heart Disease' %}
  • {% trans 'Pregnancy and Congenital Heart Disease' %}
  • {% trans 'Learning Disabilities and Congenital Heart Diseases' %}
  • {% trans 'Vocational training' %}
  • {% trans 'Informative lectures with Pediatric Cardiologists' %}
